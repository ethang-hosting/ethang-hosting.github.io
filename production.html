Misfit Theatre is proud to announce its first production, Late Company, by Jordan Tannahill [about].
Directed by Shane MacMillan and produced by Zachary Boulanger.

One year after a tragedy, two families sit down together. Far from finding the closure they seek, the dinner strips bare their good intentions to reveal layers of parental, sexual, and political hypocrisy. Written with sensitivity and humour, Late Company is both a timely and timeless meditation on a parent’s struggle to comprehend the monstrous and unknown in their child.

Late Company will take place on Wednesday February 27th through Friday March 1st, 2019 in the Saint Malachy’s High School Auditorium at 7:00 PM. Tickets are $20 each and are available at the door or online at https://misfittheatre.eventbrite.ca .
