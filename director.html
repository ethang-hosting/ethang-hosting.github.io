Shane MacMillan (Director)

Mr. MacMillan has directed a number of productions in the Saint John region over the past two decades. His work has also been presented at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe in Scotland, the Maytime Festival in Ireland and the Masan International Theatre Festival in South Korea. Shane is most noted for his interest in developing new and young talent, and has helped launch many fine careers. He is the artistic director of Youth Theatre Station and is one of the original co-founders of the Saint John Theatre Company. He also coordinates the drama program at Hampton High School where he has been a staff member since 1993.
